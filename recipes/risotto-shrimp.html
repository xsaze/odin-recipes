Risotto Shrimp

Description

Creamy risotto with shrimps, praz luk i tuiti onuiti.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Shrimps
  • Garlic
  • Prazec
  • Parsley
  • Parmegiano
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Fry the shrimps, garlic and prazec in a pot.
  2. Add water and the rice to it and start stiring.
  3. After the water is almost vaporized, add parmegiano and butter
  4. Serve with parmegiano on top
